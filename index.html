Readthechart is an educational reading demo inspired by continuous-text reading charts. This is not an official MNREAD® test and is not for diagnosis.

M-values are approximate and depend on your screen size and viewing distance.

Contrast Sensitivity Demo

Each line below has different contrast between text and background. This demo uses approximate Michelson contrast values. Results are approximate and depend on your screen settings.

    This is an educational contrast sensitivity demo. Professional contrast sensitivity tests require calibrated luminance and controlled viewing conditions. For people with low vision, high contrast materials are essential. When designing accessible content, aim for a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text.
